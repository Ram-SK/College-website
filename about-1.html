About

Our engineering college was founded in 2000 and has been providing students with quality engineering education ever since. We are located in the heart of the city and have state-of-the-art facilities.

Ram's Engineering College, an Autonomous Institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, was established in the year 2000 under the aegis of Ram's Educational Trust and Research Foundation with the motto to uplift the rural community through Technical Education. The College has grown as a reputed Institution in the past 22 years and progressing with the aim to become Centre of Excellence in Engineering Education, Research and Development.
